1. Executive Summary

How a founding SDR could work an Australian OpenAI enterprise territory

This document is designed to show how a founding SDR could work an Australian OpenAI enterprise territory: prioritise accounts, read annual reports for triggers, map use cases to strategic goals, identify buying committees, define a landing wedge, plan expansion and create personalised outbound tied to OpenAI proof points.

Territory thesis

Australia’s largest enterprises are already under pressure to use AI to improve productivity, customer experience, risk, resilience, operational efficiency and innovation. The SDR opportunity is to convert these public business priorities into high-quality, account-specific enterprise pipeline.

OpenAI proof points used: OpenAI customer stories and public OpenAI pages for Commonwealth Bank of Australia, Singular Bank, Morgan Stanley, Wayfair, STADLER, Moderna, Gradient Labs and GPT-Rosalind.

“I built this territory plan to show how I would approach the first SDR seat in Australia: prioritise accounts, read annual reports for business triggers, map use cases to strategic goals, identify the buying committee, define a landing wedge, plan expansion, anticipate objections and create personalised outbound tied to OpenAI proof points.”

Caveat

The account rankings and buying committee hypotheses are based on public annual reports and should be validated against live account ownership, current OpenAI relationships, CRM data and any active sales motions.
